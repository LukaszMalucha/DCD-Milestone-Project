Machine Learning: Regression

Overview:

Regression is a basic and commonly used type of predictive analysis. It investigates the relationship between a dependent and independent variable. Regression problem requires the prediction of a quantity, such as amounts and sizes. An output variable is a real-value - an integer or floating point value.

Real life example:

  • Examine last month's customer service calls drop.
  • Predict monthly gift card sales.
  • Predict potential clients who might default.
  • Describe impact of product price on number of sales.
